App Overview

West Coast Wonders is an app designed to help travelers explore the beauty and diversity of California by providing more affordable options for both locals and visitors of the Golden State. With features like interactive maps, trip planning, and notes for fellow travelers, West Coast Wonders is the ultimate travel companion for anyone planning a trip to the Golden State!

Notes

This feature, allows travelers to leave travel tips/advice, rating and insight to many destinations! Also allows user to post photos to share with other users that are interested.

Plan & Categories

Our "Categories" feature enables users to group their trips by way of distnace, pricing, and activity/attractions type. Trips can also be planned from user's saved or liked locations.

Map

The interactive map feature allows users to visualize destinations and attractions to plan routes and explore places of interest around the users local area or beyond.
